Event overview.

The Microsoft Identity & Network Access Engineering team invites you to attend the Identity Customer Virtual Onsite Event for Australia, New Zealand and Singapore. This three-day event is an opportunity to work closely with some members of the Identity Engineering Team and other customers on immersive engagement and collaboration.

• The event will focus on customer and engineering group discussions and collaboration on a daily basis.

• You will learn from other customers as much as from Microsoft. Therefore, it's important that attendees have a collaborative mindset and are able to share their story.

• Most attendee organizations have already deployed multiple ID/M365 services, so we'll have more advanced discussions about complex scenarios beyond the foundation already built.

Check back closer to the event date for the agenda.
